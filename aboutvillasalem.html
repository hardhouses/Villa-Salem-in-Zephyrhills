About Villa Salem

Get to Know Us: Your Next Vacation Destination in Zephyrhills

  • Book Now

Welcome to our majestic home in Salem Ave, located in the charming town of Zephyrhills! This stunning property has been completely remodeled and redesigned to create the perfect house for those who appreciate exclusivity.

Nestled in a quiet and peaceful neighborhood, our home is just a short drive away from some of the area's top attractions, including the Zephyrhills Brewing Company and the popular Zephyrhills Farmers Market. And if you're looking for outdoor activities, the nearby Zephyrhills Municipal Airport is home to skydiving and gliding, offering breathtaking views of the town and surrounding countryside.

In addition to its prime location, our home boasts over 1,930 sqft of luxurious living space, with everything feeling brand new – from the new A/C system, plumbing, and electricity to the new roof, windows, and septic system. And with a new water treatment system for the well, with a 5-year guarantee, you can enjoy fresh, clean water throughout your stay.

One of our recent guests had an excellent experience staying with us. They praised our booking process, citing how helpful our staff was, and even wrote about the welcome gift we left for them upon their arrival. We are thrilled to receive a 5-star rating from this guest and look forward to welcoming them back in the future.
