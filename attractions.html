Top Attractions Nearby

Zephyrhills Downtown Historic District

  • Zephyrhills
  • 5 minutes

The Zephyrhills Downtown Historic District is a historic district located in the center of Zephyrhills. It features various historic buildings, such as the Pasco County Bank Building, the Jeffries Block, and the City Hall. The district also has several restaurants, cafes, and shops.

Skydive City

  • Zephyrhills
  • 5 minutes

A popular skydiving center that offers a range of skydiving experiences, from tandem jumps to advanced training courses.

Epperson Lagoon

  • Wesley Chapel
  • 10 minutes

A man-made lagoon that offers crystal clear, turquoise water and a variety of water activities, such as swimming, kayaking, and paddleboarding.

Zephyrhills Museum of Military History

  • Zephyrhills
  • 10 minutes

A museum that showcases the military history of Zephyrhills and the surrounding area, featuring exhibits, artifacts, and memorabilia from various conflicts.

Hillsborough River State Park

  • Thonotosassa
  • 20 minutes

A beautiful state park with hiking trails, picnic areas, and opportunities for kayaking and canoeing. The park also features a swimming pool, a playground, and a concession stand.

Dade City

  • Dade City
  • 20 minutes

A small town with a charming historic district that features antique shops, cafes, and other small businesses. The town also offers outdoor activities, such as kayaking, horseback riding, and hiking.

Dog Training of Tampa Bay, Inc.

  • Plant City
  • 20 minutes

This is a professional dog training service located in Wesley Chapel, just a short drive from Zephyrhills. They offer a variety of training programs, including puppy training, obedience training, and behavior modification for dogs with problem behaviors. If you have a furry friend who needs some help with training, this is a great place to check out.

Dinosaur World

  • Plant City
  • 20 minutes

This outdoor park is home to over 200 life-size dinosaur replicas and features a variety of educational exhibits and interactive activities. Visitors can take a guided tour, dig for fossils, and even pan for gems. The park also has a playground, picnic area, and gift shop.

KRATE

  • Wesley Chapel
  • 20 minutes

Krate is a new indoor adventure park located at The Grove at Wesley Chapel shopping and dining destination. The park features various activities such as ninja warrior courses, trampolines, and an arcade. What makes Krate unique is that it is built with shipping containers, which gives the attraction an industrial and modern aesthetic.

Wiregrass Mall

  • Wesley Chapel
  • 20 minutes

A shopping mall that features a variety of retail stores, restaurants, and a movie theater.

Tampa Premium Outlets

  • Wesley Chapel
  • 20 minutes

An outdoor shopping center that offers a variety of high-end and designer outlet stores.

Croc Encounters

  • Tampa
  • 30 minutes

Located in nearby Tampa, this is a fun and educational attraction for all ages. At Croc Encounters, you can get up close and personal with alligators and crocodiles, as well as a variety of other reptiles. They offer guided tours, interactive experiences, and educational programs for kids and adults.

TGH Ice Plex

  • Tampa
  • 30 minutes

TGH Ice Plex is an ice skating rink, which is open year-round and offers public skating sessions, ice hockey leagues, figure skating lessons, and other events. The ice rink also features a pro shop, a snack bar, and party rooms for private events.

Hollis Garden

  • Lakeland
  • 30 minutes

Hollis Garden is a botanical garden located at 702 E Orange St, Lakeland, FL 33801. The garden features a variety of plant species, including ornamental shrubs, trees, and flowers, arranged in a formal Italian Renaissance garden style. There is also a large koi pond, a butterfly garden, and several fountains and sculptures throughout the garden.

Busch Gardens

  • Tampa
  • 35 minutes

Busch Gardens is an amusement park. The park features various thrill rides, including roller coasters, water rides, and animal exhibits with more than 12,000 animals from 300 different species. The park also has restaurants, shops, and live entertainment shows.

Adventure Island

  • Tampa
  • 35 minutes

Adventure Island is a water park, adjacent to Busch Gardens Tampa Bay. The park features water attractions, including slides, pools, and a lazy river, suitable for all ages. The park also has restaurants and shops.

Chasin Dreams Arena

  • Lakeland
  • 35 minutes

Chasin Dreams Arena is a rodeo and equestrian event center. The center hosts various events throughout the year, including bull riding, barrel racing, team roping, and other rodeo events. The center also offers horseback riding lessons and camps.

Downtown Tampa

  • Tampa
  • 35 minutes

Downtown Tampa is the central business district of Tampa. It is home to various cultural, entertainment, and dining venues, including the Tampa Riverwalk, Tampa Theatre, Florida Aquarium, Tampa Museum of Art, and many more. There are also numerous restaurants, cafes, and shops in the area.

Fort Foster State Historic Site

  • Thonotosassa
  • 35 minutes

Fort Foster State Historic Site is a historic site that features a reconstructed fort that was used during the Second Seminole War, as well as hiking trails, picnic areas, and a boat ramp.

Orbital Paintball

  • Thonotosassa
  • 40 minutes

Orbital Paintball is a paintball facility that offers various paintball fields, including woodsball, speedball, and scenario games, suitable for all skill levels. There is also a pro shop on-site.

Clearwater Beach

  • Clearwater
  • 60 minutes

A beautiful beach with soft white sand and clear blue water. The beach features numerous activities like jet skiing, parasailing, and fishing. There are also many restaurants, shops, and hotels in the area.

FloVibez Adventure Tours

  • Thonotosassa
  • 1 hour and 15 minutes

FloVibez Adventure Tours is an outdoor adventure company. It offers various tours, such as paddleboarding, kayaking, and bike tours, exploring the natural beauty of the area. There are also other water activities available, such as jet skiing and parasailing.
