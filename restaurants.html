Top Restaurants Nearby

Zephyrhills Brewing Company

  • Zephyrhills
  • 5 minutes

This microbrewery and taproom offers a variety of craft beers and pub fare. The menu includes burgers, sandwiches, salads, and snacks. The atmosphere is casual and the staff is friendly. It's located about 1 mile away from Villa Salem.

Tina & Joe's Cafe

  • Zephyrhills
  • 5 minutes

This cozy cafe offers a variety of American and Greek dishes, including breakfast items, salads, sandwiches, and gyros. The portions are generous, and the prices are reasonable. The staff is friendly, and the atmosphere is welcoming. The Greek salad, breakfast burrito, and patty melt are highly recommended.

Saigon Café

  • Zephyrhills
  • 5 minutes

This restaurant offers delicious Vietnamese cuisine with friendly service and reasonable prices. The pho, banh mi, and spring rolls are highly recommended.

Village Inn

  • 6 minutes

This restaurant is part of a chain that serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner items such as omelets, sandwiches, and pies. They also have a coffee bar and offer takeout options. It's located on US-301 in Zephyrhills.

Sonny's BBQ

  • Zephyrhills
  • 10 minutes

This barbecue restaurant offers a variety of smoked meats, including ribs, chicken, and pulled pork. The sides, such as mac and cheese and baked beans, are also tasty. The atmosphere is casual and the service is friendly. It's located about 1.5 miles from Villa Salem.

Golden Corral

  • Zephyrhills
  • 11 minutes

This buffet-style restaurant offers a wide variety of food, including breakfast items, salads, entrees, and desserts. The prices are reasonable, and the selection is good for picky eaters or large groups. It's located about 2 miles away from Villa Salem.

China Wok

  • Wesley Chapel
  • 11 minutes

This restaurant offers a variety of Chinese dishes with generous portions and affordable prices. The service is friendly, and the staff is always attentive to customers' needs. Kafe Kokopelli

  • Dade City
  • 20 minutes

This restaurant serves breakfast, lunch, and dinner, with a focus on Southern cuisine. The menu includes items such as chicken and waffles, shrimp and grits, and burgers. They also have a full bar with beer, wine, and cocktails. It's located in the historic downtown area of Zephyrhills.
